Help

To play, use the arrow keys to move around: left and right to rotate, and up and down to accelerate/deccelerate. Use the spacebar to fire, and press the r key at the appropriate times to spawn again.

NOTE: when you hit r to respawn, you will pop back into normal space immediately: if there's an asteroid in the middle of the screen, then tough luck, you've just died again. Sucker.

Points works as follows:

  • Moving around costs a flat rate of points
  • Shooting costs points
  • Shooting an asteroid pays out points
  • Merely living pays out points: the longer you stay alive, the more it pays out

This was set up to discourage spin-n-spray, and to encourage more thoughtful precision asteroid harvesting. No, we do not have a leaderboard: if people complain, then maybe one of us will take time to make one. Maybe.
